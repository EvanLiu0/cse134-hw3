Bio

Why, hello again! My name is Evan Liu, and I'm currently a third-year undergraduate student at the University of California, San Diego. I am currently pursuing a B.S. in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Machine Learning and Neural Computation, and I am also double minoring in Computer Science and Design.

My drive to pursue a career in software engineering stems from a desire to use technology to meaningfully impact others. Where others see ones and zeroes, I look for opportunities to bring people together and alleviate burdens, and I am always on the lookout for chances to use what I know about human centered design and programming to make the world a better place.
